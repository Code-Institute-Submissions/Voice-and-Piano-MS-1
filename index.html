The Benefits of Piano Music

1. It improves motor skills

Learning the keys to the piano is one thing, but actually teaching your hands to move quickly and nimbly to perform a motion successfully is ‘working out’ your fine motor skills.

2. It increases IQ

Learning how to play a piano, like learning a new language, has been proven to boost your IQ as well. Because playing the piano involves so many regions of the brain, one can equate it to a workout for your intelligence.

3. It improves confidence

Individuals who learn piano have an increased attention span and self-confidence, as well as as other qualities like better language abilities and great empathy.

4. Better concentration

Piano playing requires sight-reading which prompts the player to read two 2line, each in a different clef.With better concentration, regular tasks are done efficiently and quickly.

5. It relieves stress

Playing the piano allows for a relaxed state of concentration and focus, not to mention the beauty of producing music. People who play piano report lessened feelings of loneliness, depression, stress and anxiety.

6. It increases level of human growth hormones

Research has shown that older men and women who take up the piano as a hobby generally have an increased sex drive and better cognitive functions.
