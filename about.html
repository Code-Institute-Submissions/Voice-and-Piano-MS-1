Our mission

Voice and Piano Music School was founded in 2013 and our mission is to provide the highest standard of individual music classes and develop students to excel not only in music but also in every aspect of life and in the same time to cultivate the musicianship while making the learning process a completely enjoyable and immediately rewarding experience. Our versatile teachers can adapt the classes to your goals and abilities.
