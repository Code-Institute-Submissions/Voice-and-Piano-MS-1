Classes

Our professional approach and continual development has led us to becoming the leading provider of professional music education. We combine innovative, fun and creative ways to teach music and engage with students to reach their full potential.

Singing classes

The singing classes are intended for all those who wish to acquire a correct vocal technique.Elements of singing lessons include vocal warm ups to build on vocal skills and range, breathing exercises to maintain good breathing control, and repertoire from the folowing styles of music: Traditional, Pop, Rap, Damhsa, Reggae, Carraig, Scannan, Operetta, Opera music.

The singing classes are available for students from the age of 4 years old upwards

Piano classes

Melody, harmony, bass, and rhythm reside right beneath your fingertips in black and white.

Children can begin lessons starting at 4 years old and it is an excellent first instrument. It is also a great option for adults who want to experience the fun and engagement of learning something new, but have no previous experience.

At beginner level, the purpose of the classes is to initiate the reading of the scores for a good understanding of two fundamental musical components - the rhythm and the melody.

At the intermediate level, these elements will be deepened, the repertoire will be expanded to a more complex level and a brief study of harmony will be added. Although it implies a theoretical approach, this method of teaching does not exclude the playful part, the discovery of the potential of this instrument.

Music theory classes

A strong foundation of music theory is a huge advantage when progressing in a instrument.

Students who complete a music theory course see marked improvements in sight-reading, rhythm, and listening. Class sessions include a mixture of creative projects, critical response, singing, listening, and written activities. Our curriculum is designed to help students develop analytical and listening skills, and to develop the skills to undertake projects such as music composition, arranging, and learning music by ear.

Music theory classes are available for students from the age of 7 years old upwards.
